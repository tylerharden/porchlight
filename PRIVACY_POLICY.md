# Porchlight Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 17, 2026

Porchlight is designed to run locally on your Mac.

Porchlight inspects local listening ports and process metadata so it can show development servers. This may include port numbers, process names, commands, working directories, server status, pinned/recent state, and optional group/icon settings. This information is stored locally on your device using app preferences and Porchlight configuration/state files.

Porchlight does not create an account, track usage, include third-party analytics, or send your local server/process data to Tyler Harden or any analytics service.

If you choose to open GitHub links, report issues, download releases, or interact with the project repository, GitHub may process information according to GitHub's own terms and privacy policy. If Porchlight is installed through the Mac App Store, Apple may process purchase, download, crash, or diagnostics information according to Apple's policies and your device settings.

You can remove Porchlight's local state by deleting its app preferences and files under `~/.config/porchlight` and `~/.local/state/porchlight`.
